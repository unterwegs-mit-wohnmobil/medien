1.Hyong - Chon-Ji Hyong

Chon-Ji stands for sky and earth. The name is a symbol for the beginning of something new. In Asia it stands for the genesis of the earth and the beginning of mankind.

built by: J.Fischer

last change: 2.10.2012

Description of the movements

The position of the shoulder is represented by a rounded, black rectangle with an oval lying inside.  The position of the hip is illustrated by a rounded, blue rectangle.
