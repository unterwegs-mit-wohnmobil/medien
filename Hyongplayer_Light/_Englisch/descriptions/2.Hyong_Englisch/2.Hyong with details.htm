2.Hyong - Tan-Gun Hyong

In the Korean legend Tan-Gun was the son of a God and a human woman. He founded the present Korea in the year 2333 BC.

The movements will be described in English in the next version.

Description of the movements

The position of the shoulder is represented by a rounded, black rectangle with an oval lying inside.  The position of the hip is illustrated by a rounded, blue rectangle.
