HyongPlayer by Johann Fischer

purpose

Taekwondo step sequences are illustrated in a two-dimensional view (from above).

This application is suitable for supporting and practicing the foot, hand, shoulder, head and hip positions of the movements of Hyongs or other step sequences.
New forms should not be learnt by using this application.

For the (sensible) usage of the application basic knowledge in traditional Taekwondo are necessary, the used concepts are presupposed and not explained.

The following step sequences are implemented:

  • 1.Hyong
  • 2.Hyong
  • 3.Hyong
